What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy Cricket is an online gaming platform which works in co-ordination with the actual(live) cricket game. There are multiple fantasy cricket platforms which host multiple fantasy contest in which the user can participate by creating a team of 11 players out of the 22 players playing in the match. Based on the performance of the cricketers the fantasy contestant’s team are rated and ultimately the contestant with the best team is rewarded with cash price. For more reference, click here!

What does our platform do?

The Optimized team selection algorithm” is a Machine Learning (ML) based model which uses the historical data of all the IPL matches to predict the performance of the players in the upcoming match. Hence, here we are using the computers computational power for calculating the complex statistical data and human’s analytical skill to select the important features for predicting player’s performance. Using the results of the ML model we recommend you the most optimal team to participate in the fantasy cricket.
